|Procedure|Step-by-step instructions for performing a task consistently|Change management steps, Onboarding/offboarding|
|Guidelines|Recommended best practices (less rigid than standards)|Coding style guidelines, Backup recommendations|

## 2. Compliance

### Definition

Compliance is the adherence to laws, regulations, standards, and internal policies.  
In IT, it ensures systems and processes meet security and regulatory requirements.

---

### Purpose of Compliance

1. Legal Obligations â†’ Avoid fines, sanctions, or litigation
    
2. Trust & Reputation â†’ Demonstrates organizational reliability
    
3. Data Protection â†’ Safeguards customer and employee information
    
4. Business Continuity â†’ Ensures operations continue during disruptions
    

---

### Compliance Activities

1. Monitoring & Reporting
    
    - Track adherence to policies and regulations
        
    - Identify gaps and remediate them promptly
        
2. Due Diligence & Due Care
    
    - Due diligence: Careful evaluation before engaging in activities
        
    - Due care: Ongoing actions to protect the organization
        
3. Internal vs External Compliance
    
    - Internal: Self-assessments, internal audits
        
    - External: Regulatory audits, independent reviews
        
4. Automation in Compliance
    
    - Use tools for continuous monitoring, reporting, and alerts
        

---

### Consequences of Noncompliance

- Fines and legal sanctions
