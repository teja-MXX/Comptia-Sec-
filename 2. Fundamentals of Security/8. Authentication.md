## 1. Prevent Unauthorized Access

Authentication stops attackers from accessing systems by forcing them to prove identity.

- Servers    
- Databases
- Cloud apps
- Network shares
    
Analogy:  
Only real employees (not imposters) can enter the office floor.

This ensures operations run safely and remain trustworthy.
